🚀 Advantages of Our System

  • 🩺 Predictive Health Monitoring: Identifies risks before symptoms occur, empowering preventive healthcare strategies.
  • 📲 Real-Time Doctor Notifications: Physicians receive instant updates, enabling faster clinical decisions and life-saving interventions.
  • 🏥 Reduced Healthcare Burden: Decreases unnecessary hospital admissions, freeing up healthcare resources for critical cases.
  • 📈 Enhanced Patient Engagement: Patients feel reassured knowing their devices are continuously monitored.
  • 🔒 Maximum Data Privacy: End-to-end encrypted communication safeguards personal health information.

In the future, the system can integrate additional wearables and biosensors, further expanding its capabilities into broader cardiovascular care.
