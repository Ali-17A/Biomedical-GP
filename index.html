Welcome to Our Graduation Project 🎓

📋 Project Title: Designing Workflow Models for Medical Device Failure Reporting using Digital Twins for Pacemaker Monitoring.

💡 Concept: Pacemakers are critical life-saving devices. However, traditional monitoring methods can delay early detection of failures. Our solution introduces Digital Twin technology to establish a continuous, real-time, intelligent monitoring system for pacemakers, improving patient safety and healthcare outcomes.

🧠 By developing a digital replica of the physical pacemaker and connecting it through IoT networks, real-time performance data is continuously compared to expected behavior. Artificial Intelligence algorithms detect anomalies early, ensuring proactive interventions rather than reactive treatments.

🔍 Main Features:

  • 🌐 IoT Connectivity: Enabling real-time remote health monitoring.
  • 🧠 AI-Powered Anomaly Detection: Predicting failures before they occur.
  • 🛡️ End-to-End Security: Protecting sensitive medical data with encryption protocols (HIPAA Compliant).
  • 📈 Feedback Loop: Doctors receive instant alerts, enabling timely decisions.

🏆 Objective: Revolutionise medical device monitoring using smart digital replicas to improve reliability, safety, and personalised patient care.
