Introduction

Pacemakers are life-saving devices, but traditional monitoring methods can miss early failures. Our project integrates Digital Twin technology to monitor pacemakers in real-time and predict failures before they happen.

Technology Overview

Digital Twin: A virtual replica of the pacemaker.

IoT Sensors: Collect real-time heart and device data.

AI: Detects anomalies and predicts failures.

Cybersecurity: Protects patient data and ensures device safety.

System Design

Our system collects real-time data from pacemaker sensors, sends it to the Digital Twin via IoT, processes it with AI to detect anomalies, and alerts doctors in case of any critical failures.

Advantages Over Traditional Monitoring

  • Real-time failure prediction
  • Immediate doctor alerts
  • Reduced hospital visits
  • Improved patient safety

Challenges and Future Scope

Our project must address cybersecurity, comply with medical standards like ISO 14708-1 and HIPAA, and ensure ethical handling of patient data. Future research can expand Digital Twin applications to other devices.
