🛠️ System Design

Our system is structured into interconnected layers, forming a complete and secure monitoring loop:

  1. 📥 Data Collection Layer: Pacemaker sensors collect physiological and operational metrics.
  2. 🌐 Communication Layer: IoT modules securely transmit data to the Digital Twin infrastructure via wireless channels.
  3. 🧠 AI Processing Layer: Incoming real-time data is continuously analyzed using AI models trained on normal and failure conditions.
  4. 🚨 Alert and Action Layer: Detected anomalies trigger alerts sent directly to physicians, prompting timely intervention.
  5. 🔄 Continuous Learning: Data from resolved cases are fed back into the AI models, making them smarter over time (self-improvement).

In addition, cloud-based storage ensures secure historical data analysis for trend identification and research purposes.
