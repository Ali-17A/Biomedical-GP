🔬 Technology Overview

The successful deployment of our system relies on an integration of several state-of-the-art technologies:

  • 🧩 Digital Twin Technology: A virtual dynamic model of the pacemaker that mirrors its real-time condition, performance, and behaviour.
  • 📡 Internet of Things (IoT): Tiny embedded sensors gather heart signals, pacing events, battery status, and transmit data securely to cloud servers.
  • 🧠 Artificial Intelligence (AI): Machine learning models process vast datasets to identify deviations from normal operation patterns, detecting even subtle anomalies invisible to human observation.
  • 🛡️ Cybersecurity Architecture: All data transmission and storage follow strict medical regulations, including ISO 14708-1 and HIPAA, ensuring full compliance and safety.

This integration allows real-time visibility of device health, making predictive healthcare achievable.
