⚠️ Challenges and Future Scope

While the Digital Twin approach is promising, several challenges remain:

  • 🔒 Cybersecurity Risks: Medical devices have become targets for cyberattacks; rigorous security testing and encryption are essential.
  • 📜 Regulatory Compliance: Navigating complex frameworks such as ISO 14708-1, HIPAA, and FDA requirements poses significant hurdles.
  • 🔋 Power Management: IoT modules and sensors must maintain ultra-low power consumption to avoid frequent surgeries for battery replacements.
  • 🧠 AI Bias and Validation: Machine Learning models must be extensively validated to ensure no bias or false alarms in anomaly detection.
  • 🤔 Ethical and Legal Issues: Managing ownership, transparency, and consent for medical digital replicas requires new ethical frameworks.

🌟 Future Vision: Our system lays the groundwork for fully autonomous "smart hospitals," where patient devices continuously communicate with healthcare systems for predictive, proactive, and personalized medical care.
