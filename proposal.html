üéØ Objective

Pacemakers are Class III life-sustaining medical devices. Traditional monitoring through periodic check-ins often fails to detect early signs of issues such as battery drainage, lead fracture, or malfunction. Our project proposes the integration of Digital Twin (DT) technology for real-time monitoring, predictive failure detection, and automated alerts.

By creating a virtual replica of a pacemaker, continuously fed data through IoT sensors, and interpreted with AI, we enable proactive intervention, enhanced patient safety, and responsive care. This project builds on existing studies and introduces full DT integration ‚Äî real-time AI-based predictions, remote alerts, and evolving patient-specific learning.
